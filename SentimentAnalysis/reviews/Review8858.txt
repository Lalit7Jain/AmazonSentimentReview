I have not yet explored all it can do. But I have enabled some pretty cool skills. It is important to note that she doesn't come immediately with all the skills. You use the Alexa App to enable certain skills that are of interest to you. Some of my favorites so far is the Mixologist Skill and My Chef. With the Mixologist skill you can have Alexa tell you how to make specific drinks and My Chef will list ingredients and give you recipes.
