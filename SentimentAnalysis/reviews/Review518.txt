Really neat and fun gadget for your home. Some really neat things you can do with it  if you want to get some extra gadgets to go with it. With some other gadgets you can hook it up to your home's lights and you can tell it to turn on the lights in whichever room when you walk in. You can link it with spotify to play songs or playlists off there. And so many other things you can do with it as well.
