Echo comes out of the box just waiting to be amazing. You start by plugging it in, and from there you get instructions from Alexa on how to pair with your internet. Download the Echo app and you're off. So far, the voice recognition is spot on. There aren't many instances where she hasn't understood what I was asking for. If you have your music REALLY loud, sometimes you need to raise your voice to her, but she can still hear you.
